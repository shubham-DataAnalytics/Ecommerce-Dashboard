# E-Commerce Performance Dashboard using Power BI

## ❓ Problem Statement
The objective of this project is to analyze e-commerce sales data and evaluate business performance across revenue, profit, and quantity sold. The goal is to identify key contributors to profit, understand customer and payment behavior, and support data-driven decision-making for business growth.

---

## 📊 Overview
This dashboard presents a comprehensive analysis of e-commerce performance, focusing on revenue, profit, and sales distribution across categories, customers, payment modes, and geographic regions.

---

## 📌 Key KPI
- **Total Revenue:** ₹438K  
- **Total Profit:** ₹37K  
- **Total Quantity Sold:** 5615 units  

---

## 🔨 Process
1. **Data Modeling**  
   - Structured sales data with relationships between orders, products, customers, and regions  
   - Enabled filtering by state and order date range (2018)  

2. **DAX Measures**  
   - Created measures for revenue, profit, and quantity  
   - Built calculations for profit breakdown across category, subcategory, customer, and payment mode  

3. **Visualizations**  
   - Profit analysis by category and subcategory  
   - Customer and city-level performance  
   - Payment mode distribution  
   - State-wise profit contribution

## 🎯 Key Insights

- **Clothing, Electronics, and Furniture are the Top Profit-Contributing Categories**  
  Focus on expanding inventory and running targeted promotions in these categories to maximize revenue growth.

- **Printers, Bookcases, and Sarees Lead in Subcategory Profit**  
  Increase stock availability and prioritize marketing campaigns for these high-performing subcategories.

- **Top Customers Contribute Higher Profit**  
  Implement loyalty programs, personalized offers, and retention strategies to maintain and grow high-value customers.

- **Credit Card and COD are the Most Used Payment Modes**  
  Ensure seamless transaction experience for these payment methods and consider offering incentives (cashback/discounts) to boost conversions.

- **Profit Distribution Varies Across States**  
  Strengthen operations and marketing in high-performing states like Madhya Pradesh and Maharashtra while identifying opportunities to improve underperforming regions.

- **Profit Distribution Varies Across Cities**  
  Focus on high-performing cities for expansion while using targeted campaigns to improve sales in lower-performing areas.
